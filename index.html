KIDS - Kannadi Integreted Charitable Development Society

A child with any disability always finds it difficult to perform his/her activities of daily life. This include self care, household management, social relationship & studies in school.

Normally these children will be rehabilitated for their specific problem. Eg: A mentally challenged child will be normally rehabilitated with the help of special education, psychotherapy mainly and to certain level of medication also help them,but for physically challenged,physiotherapy is more important.

Our social sense must make us to behave and act in a responsive manner to the necessities of these needy children. KIDS a charitable institution for the welfare of such differently able children is a response of some social activities and parents of these unfortunate children.
