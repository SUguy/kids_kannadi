KIDS - Kannadi Integreted Charitable Development Society

Objectives of KIDS Special School

  1. Impart special training to the challenged children for the development for their mental and physical faculties.
  2. Making them capable of attending to their day to day activities
  3. The importance of early identification and rehabilation of these special children.
  4. Create awareness among the public against the neglect and discrimination of special children.
  5. Persuade the public to accept their responsibility to such children and encourage them to participate in this noble venture in positive manner.
  6. Render service of special medical personnel to the needy in the institution.
  7. Intiate, develop, and maintain effective rehabilation facilities.
